Green Park Restaurant

India

Green Park is a multi-cuisine restaurant, located in a serene environment, which has everything from dining area, garden area, banquet hall, bar and even a pool side seating arrangement for those who prefer the outdoor environment.

Green Park also facilitates a small party area right on top of the pool, which can house 25 guests with ease.
